How We Work

Retained search.
Founder-run.
Every time.

We don't do contingency. We don't hand your search to a junior. Every engagement is run personally — with full accountability from brief to close.

We embed into your technical context — model architecture, consensus design, protocol stack — so we evaluate candidates the way your own engineering team would. Not from a job spec. From understanding.

We maintain active maps of AI researchers, L1/L2 protocol engineers, and DeFi builders globally. We know who is shipping, who might move, and what it would take. Most of our best candidates aren't applying anywhere.
